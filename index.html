A little bit of who I am

I was born in Argentina, and currently live in the beautiful city of Wroclaw in Poland.

As a passionate illustrator and designer, I bring characters and ideas to life through a blend of vibrant creativity and technical precision. With a deep love for storytelling and visual expression, I specialize in crafting detailed illustrations, high definition 3D characters, animations, and other creative personal projects and aspirations, such as tattooing and UI design. My work ranges from conceptual sketches to fully realized digital creations, reflecting my dedication to both artistry and innovation.
