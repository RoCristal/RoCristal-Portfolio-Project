About me

A little bit of who I am.

I was born in Argentina, and currently live in the beautiful city of Wroclaw in Poland.

I am passionate about technology, enjoy taking new challenges and expand my knowledge. I am a front end developer currently learning React as part of my web development journey.
Looking forward to having more experience and projects.

My current position as robotics automation support, allowed me to start to be in contact with code, this sparked my curiosity and suddenly I found myself dedicating my evenings to learning how to code.

I truly enjoy the process of problem-solving and creation. Seeing a few lines slowly becoming something beautiful gives me a great satisfaction.

When I am not learning how to code (or working) I am enjoying a peaceful walk with my two pug dogs. They love any type of food and long walks, you can see a lovely picture of them in this HTML web site I also created.

On the weekends I love watching a good horror or suspense movie and cook sweet popcorn to enjoy it.
I also love to travel and get to see amazing landscapes and cities.

Since I was little I was drawn to art and creativity so I also try to add some drawings or tattoos to my daily tasks as you can see in my art website. , so I feel like web development allows me explore my creative side in a different way.
